"Slovenia’s second-largest city is a winter haven for skiers, a summer getaway for hikers and an all-year stop for fans of Slovenian wine. The marquee attraction is the world’s oldest vine. The plant, along with a small museum and wine bar, can be found at the Old Vine House.Other key spots for oenophiles include, a wine boutique with a massive cellar that visitors can tour, and Rozmarin restaurant, which serves Slovenian and international food — lamb carpaccio with dandelion pesto, red mullet baked in a paper bag — and scores of Slovenian wines."
