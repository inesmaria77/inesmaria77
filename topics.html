Maribor

beyond the crowds and city lights



Besides the usual hanging spots for students, what is worth seeing is: Lent by river Drava Mariborski otok (great bathing spot in the summer) Pohorje Kalvarija (nice view over entire Maribor). There is quite a selection of nightlife in Maribor and it is dispersed throughout the entire bank of river Drava. You can find varieties of food from traditional Slovene to restaurants with Italian, Mexican, Chinese, Indian and even vegan cusine. Maribor is one of my favorite cities - not just because of the city, its structure and what it offers, but most of all, because of the people. More about Maribor
